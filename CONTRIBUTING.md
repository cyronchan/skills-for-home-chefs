# Contributing to Skills for Home Chefs

Thank you for wanting to contribute! This project is built by home cooks, for home cooks.

You do not need to be a professional chef or a developer. If you cook at home and have something worth sharing, you belong here.

---

## What you can contribute

### 🍳 A recipe skill
A dish you cook regularly, written in the skill format. This is the most valuable contribution.
See [templates/RECIPE-SKILL-TEMPLATE.md](templates/RECIPE-SKILL-TEMPLATE.md) to get started.

### 🧠 A cooking skill
A practical workflow, technique, or piece of chef thinking that helps people cook better.
See existing skills in `skills/` for the format.

### 🌏 A localised skill
A recipe or cooking skill adapted for a specific region, culture, or seasonal context.
Example: `skills/recipes/cantonese/steamed-fish/SKILL.md`

### 🐛 A fix or improvement
Spotted a mistake, an unclear step, or a missing safety note? Open an issue or submit a fix.

---

## How to add a recipe skill

1. **Fork** this repository.
2. Create a new folder: `skills/recipes/[cuisine]/[dish-name]/`
3. Copy [templates/RECIPE-SKILL-TEMPLATE.md](templates/RECIPE-SKILL-TEMPLATE.md) into that folder and rename it `SKILL.md`.
4. Fill in the template with your recipe.
5. Add your recipe to the cuisine section of [skills/recipes/README.md](skills/recipes/README.md).
6. Open a **Pull Request** with a short description of the dish and why you love it.

### Naming conventions
- Folder names: lowercase, hyphenated (`steamed-egg-with-minced-pork`)
- Cuisine folders: lowercase, one word or hyphenated (`cantonese`, `japanese`, `south-asian`, `western`, `fusion`)
- One dish per folder

## Recipe skill standards

Every recipe skill must:

- ✅ Be something you have actually cooked and eaten
- ✅ Use the [recipe skill template](templates/RECIPE-SKILL-TEMPLATE.md)
- ✅ Include a **Taste like a chef** section
- ✅ State clearly if any ingredient is essential vs optional
- ✅ Include a food safety note if there is raw meat, seafood, eggs, or reheating involved
- ✅ Be written for a home kitchen — no restaurant equipment assumed

A recipe skill must not:
- ❌ Require specialist equipment without offering an alternative
- ❌ Use vague measurements for steps where precision matters
- ❌ Shame any ingredient, budget, or skill level
- ❌ Be copied from a copyrighted source without original adaptation

## Community values

This project welcomes cooks of all backgrounds, budgets, kitchens, and experience levels.

- Every cuisine and home cooking tradition is equally valued.
- Budget-friendly, accessible, and waste-conscious cooking is celebrated.
- We prioritise recipes that are practical for real home kitchens.
- Be kind and constructive in feedback — we are all learning.
